Getting started

What does Griz actually do?

Griz reads the conversation you're in and drafts three replies in a tone you pick (Flirty, Playful, Witty, Direct, or Warm). Two ways to use it:

  • In any app — switch to the Griz keyboard inside Hinge, Tinder, Bumble, iMessage, or any text field, and tap Generate.
  • In the Griz app — drop a screenshot, pick a tone, pick a reply. You can also describe the situation in words if you don't have a screenshot handy.
How do I enable the Griz keyboard?

After installing Griz, open iOS Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Add New Keyboard and pick Griz. Then tap Griz in the list and turn on Allow Full Access.

Full Access is required because iOS blocks any network request from a keyboard that doesn't have it — which means the AI can't see the conversation or generate a reply without it. Griz only reads the text near your cursor when you tap Generate. We don't log keystrokes.

Why does Griz need Photos access?

So you can pick a chat screenshot and, optionally, so the home screen of the Griz app can show "Use the screenshot you just took" as a shortcut. We never browse, index, or upload anything until you explicitly tap Generate on a specific image. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

Subscriptions & billing

How does pricing work?

Griz is a paid app. Two plans, both with a free trial — pick one when you open the app for the first time:

  • Weekly with 3-day trial — try Griz for three days. Cancel anytime in iOS Settings before the trial ends and you won't be charged.
  • Monthly — billed monthly, no trial.

Either plan unlocks unlimited replies, all five tones, the keyboard, the screenshot coach, and every feature we ship going forward. Exact prices show on the paywall in your local currency before you commit.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through the App Store, so you manage them in iOS Settings:

  1. Open iOS Settings and tap your name at the top.
  2. Tap Subscriptions.
  3. Find Griz in the list and tap Cancel Subscription.

You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. We can't cancel or refund subscriptions on our end — Apple handles that. Request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com.

How do I restore a purchase on a new phone?

Open Griz → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to subscribe. Your subscription will reconnect automatically.

Can I try Griz before paying?

Yes — the weekly plan starts with a 3-day free trial. You get full access to every feature for those three days. Cancel any time before the trial ends in iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions, and you won't be charged.

Privacy & safety

Do you store my screenshots or messages?

No. Screenshots and text you submit pass through our server just long enough to call the AI model, and are then discarded. They are not written to disk and are not logged.

On your device, the Griz app saves your generation history so you can refer back to it. You can delete individual conversations from the Messages tab, or remove all of it by deleting the app.

Will my data be used to train AI models?

No. We use Anthropic's commercial API, under which API content is not used to train Anthropic's models. We don't train any model of our own on user data either.

Does the Griz keyboard log my keystrokes?

No. The Griz keyboard only reads the text around your cursor — up to 500 characters before and after — and only at the moment you tap Generate. It doesn't log what you type, it doesn't read passwords or secure fields (iOS blocks that anyway), and it doesn't send anything in the background.

Is Griz safe to use on my regular keyboard or should I switch?

Griz is an additional keyboard you can switch to with the globe icon. Your default keyboard stays installed. When you're in a sensitive field — like a password manager — iOS automatically switches away from third-party keyboards regardless. Use Griz when you want AI help and the normal Apple keyboard the rest of the time.

Troubleshooting

"Couldn't reach Griz. Try again." — what do I do?

This error means the app couldn't talk to our server. Usually one of:

  • You're offline or on a spotty connection — try again on Wi-Fi.
  • Full Access is off on the Griz keyboard — enable it in Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Griz.
  • Our server is briefly down — check griz.app or email us if it lasts more than a few minutes.
The replies don't sound like me.

Two things to try. First, switch tone — Flirty, Playful, Witty, Direct, and Warm each pull the style in a different direction; the wrong tone is usually the culprit. Second, drop a few of your own real texts into the app's voice samples; Griz uses them to match your slang, rhythm, and length.

And remember Griz is a drafting tool — the best use is as a springboard. Take the draft that's closest to what you'd say, tweak the phrasing, hit send.

How do I delete my data?

Because Griz doesn't have user accounts, everything tied to you lives either on your device or as an anonymous device UUID that resets daily. To clear everything:

  1. Delete the Griz app (removes the device UUID, local history, settings).
  2. Optionally cancel your subscription in App Store settings.

If you want us to manually purge the device UUID from our quota cache, email us with the UUID shown in the app's Settings screen.
