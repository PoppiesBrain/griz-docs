How Griz works.

Three steps, under thirty seconds from stuck to sent.

1

Screenshot or switch

Screenshot the chat that's stuck, or switch to the Griz keyboard in any text field.

2

Pick a tone

Flirty, playful, witty, direct, or warm. I match the vibe — yours and theirs.

3

Send it

Three drafts in your voice. Tap to insert, edit to taste, hit send.

Your chats are yours.

Screenshots are processed for the generation, then discarded — never stored. We don't train models on your conversations, we don't sell data, and there are no ads or trackers anywhere in the app.
